# Refund Policy

BugIt is a licensed product from Taskivator. We want you to be able to get BugIt
working, and this policy explains when a purchase can be refunded.

## 7-Day Refund Window

You may request a refund within **7 days of purchase** if any of the following apply:

- BugIt does not work as advertised
- A reproducible technical issue prevents normal use
- Support cannot resolve the issue within a reasonable timeframe

We may ask for a short description of the problem and the steps you have already
tried, so that support can confirm the issue or help you resolve it first.

## When a Refund Is Not Available

Refunds are generally **not** available for:

- Change of mind
- Purchasing by mistake
- Failure to meet the documented system requirements
- Requests submitted after the 7-day window has passed

Before buying, please review the system requirements — Visual Studio Code, an AI
provider (GitHub Copilot or your own OpenAI or Anthropic key), and a supported
version of Python. BugIt does not include an AI model or subscription.

## What Happens When a Refund Is Approved

When we approve a refund, the purchase is reversed and the associated license is
retired. Specifically:

- The associated license is permanently revoked
- Future activations with that license stop
- Future updates for that license stop
- Support tied to that license ends

## How to Request a Refund

Email **[support@bugit.dev](mailto:support@bugit.dev)** from the account used for
the purchase, within 7 days, and describe the problem you ran into. Please do not
include confidential source code, customer data, private tickets, or secrets in
your message. We will confirm the outcome by email.

This policy covers refunds only and does not limit any rights you may have under
applicable consumer-protection law.
